My Best Friend Cocoa

Cocoa's Character

Cocoa was more than a pet to our faimly, he was a brother and son! Cocoa was the best dog a family could ask for. He was truly one of a kind.

What made Cocoa so Special?

  • Loyal to a Fault
    • Many accounts Cocoa would lie down on our driveway waiting for mom to come home to return for hours.
    • Cocoa use to cross country ski with us for hours on end. Eventhough midway through he'd be carrying an extra 5lbs in snowballs attached to his body.
  • Kind and Friendly
    • Cocoa would rarely ever bark and never bite anybody.
  • Always Happy
    • Cocoa had an Aura around him that was unmatched. Even the way he walked made it seem like he was happy. I use to call his walk "the puddy walk".
